Blueberry Lemonade Martini

Home

Description

This blueberry lemonade martini is sweet, tart, cool, and refreshingly delicious. The flavors say summer, but the color might make you think holidays.

Ingredients

  • 2 fluid ounces lemon infused vodka
  • 2 fluid ounces blueberry lemonade
  • ½ fluid ounce agave syrup or simple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 wedge plus 1 slice lemon or lime, divided, for garnish
  • chili lime seasoning, such as Tajin® (optional)

Steps

  1. Add vodka, blueberry lemonade, agave syrup, and lime juice to a cocktail shaker.
  2. Fill the cocktail shaker with ice and shake until the outside is frosted, about 30 seconds.
  3. Sprinkle Tajin seasoning on a plate, rub the rim of your favorite glass with a lemon or lime wedge, and dip the glass in seasoning to coat the rim. Strain the cocktail into the glass.
